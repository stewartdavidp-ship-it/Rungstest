Rungs v1.0.10
=============
Release Date: January 24, 2026

IMPROVED REORDER CONTROLS
-------------------------
Redesigned the arrow buttons for reordering pairs in the stack:
- Up arrow (▲) now on the LEFT side - moves pair up
- Down arrow (▼) now on the RIGHT side - moves pair down
- Larger touch targets (28px vs 24px)
- Better visual clarity with left/right positioning
- Updated tutorial and intro text to explain new layout

FIX: "I KNOW THE RULES" FLOW
----------------------------
- Added extra safeguard to prevent "How to Play" intro from 
  appearing after clicking "I know the rules"
- skipIntroAfterTutorial flag now set during skip flow
